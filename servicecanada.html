Service Canada

Service Canada is the place where Canadians go—whether online, by phone, or in person—to access a wide range of programs, services, and benefits they need from the Government of Canada and its many partners.

Topics

Employment Insurance (EI)

Includes information about EI, how to apply for EI, how to complete your EI report, how to access My Service Canada Account, and reporting fraud.

Canada Pension Plan (CPP)

Includes information about the CPP, how to apply for CPP, disability and survivor benefits, pension sharing, and credit splitting.

Old Age Security (OAS) Programs

Includes information about how to apply for OAS, the Guaranteed Income Supplement (GIS), the Allowance for people aged 60 to 64, and the Allowance for the Survivor.

Social Insurance Number (SIN)

Includes information about the SIN, how to apply for a SIN, keep your SIN safe, and what to do if your SIN is lost or stolen.

Passports

Includes information about Passports, application forms, and step-by-step guides on how to apply for a Canadian passport.

Apprenticeship Incentive Grant (AIG)

Includes information about the AIG, how to apply for AIG, the supporting documents that are required, and deadlines.

Wage Earner Protection Program (WEPP)

Includes eligibility information for the WEPP, how to apply for WEPP, financial details related to WEPP, tax slips, and dates and deadlines.

Employer Contact Centre

Includes contact information for assistance with Records of Employment (ROE), ROE Web, Report on Hirings, Temporary Foreign Worker Program and more.
